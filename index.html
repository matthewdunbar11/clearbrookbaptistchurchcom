Reaching the Roanoke Valley

Adults

Whether you're a college-aged student, a senior citizen adult, or somewhere in between, you'll fit in just fine. We have Sunday School classes for both college students and also adults. as well as many activities such as the ladies' fellowship lunches, yearly church picnic, gospel sings, and much more.

Teens

Teens have their own Sunday School as well as Wednesday night program. The teen program has both sound Biblical teaching and also fun activities such as Church Camp and much more than we could possibly list.

Children

Children have their own Sunday School, Children's Church, and Wednesday night program where they are taught the foundations of the Bible. The Wednesday night program is a time of fun, games, and Bible teaching.

Clearbrook Baptist Church is a place to call home

Our church is a welcoming place for believers to assemble to worship God and encourage each other. The Bible is our foundation in everything we believe and preach. We are a missions-minded congregation who believes that missions (both foreign and local) is the very heart beat of God, and we consider ourselves old fashioned, Independent, and Fundamental. Our church is a country church where God is doing great and mighty things. We would LOVE to have you and your family join us for a worship service. May the Lord Jesus Christ richly bless you in all that you do!
